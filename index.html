Welcome to the Flickr Tickler

The Flickr Tickler is a unique way to view flickr pictures quickly and smoothly.

Using your keyboard (j to go down, k to go up - even across pages!), you can look at any group / set of flickr pictures you want. Group pools, personal photostreams, even tags!

To get started, put any flickr url in this box and we'll take it from there. If you want to do a tag search, type one or more in the box instead. (Currently only users and groups are working)
